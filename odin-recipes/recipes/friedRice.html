Fried Rice

Description

Traditionally Chinese dish but has its own flavor in every delicacy

Ingredients

  • Leftover rice
  • Eggs
  • Veggies
  • Oil of choice
  • Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Onions
  • Salt,pepper,etc.

Steps

  1. Heat the woke and add oil
  2. Saute the diced onion and add salt, spies and MSG
  3. break the eggs on side and fry it
  4. Add Soya sauce and vingar
  5. Finally add rice and toss it periodically
