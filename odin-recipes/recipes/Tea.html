Tea

Description

British drink but now daily routine for South Asians

Ingredients

  • Tea
  • Milk
  • Cardmamom
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Put milk on boil in one pot
  2. Put water in another pot and turn on the stove
  3. Add Ginger, tea, Cardmamom and wait for it to boil
  4. Add the boiled milk and sugar
  5. Wait until reddish-brown color
